Color & Blend Control Panel

Blend modes control how colors interact when they overlap:

  • Normal: No blending, top color covers bottom color
  • Multiply: Darkens colors, similar to overlapping color filters
  • Screen: Lightens colors, similar to projecting multiple images
  • Overlay: Combines Multiply and Screen, enhances contrast
  • Darken/Lighten: Selects the darker/lighter color at each pixel
  • Color Dodge/Burn: Brightens/darkens based on the blend color
  • Hard/Soft Light: Similar to harsh/diffused spotlight effects
  • Difference/Exclusion: Creates inverted color effects
  • Hue/Saturation/Color/Luminosity: Blends specific color components

The examples above demonstrate the selected blend mode effect.
